Privacy Policy

At Vavi Table Water Company, we respect your privacy. Below is our Privacy Policy regarding the use of our App and Services:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. The only information we receive is your booking order submitted via our app.
  • WhatsApp Booking: After clicking "Submit Order", you will be redirected to our official WhatsApp number to complete your booking.
  • Geographic Restriction: We only accept and fulfill orders within Sokoto State, Nigeria.
  • No Pre-payment: We do not request or collect any payment until your order is delivered to you.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your information with any third parties, advertisers, or external platforms.
  • No Cookies or Tracking: Our app does not use cookies, tracking tools, or analytics services to monitor your activity.
  • Children's Privacy: Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18 without parental or guardian supervision.
  • Secure Communication: All order communications happen directly through WhatsApp, which uses end-to-end encryption.
  • Policy Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us through our official WhatsApp line +234 803 692 7662.
